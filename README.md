    <h2>Chia Pudding</h2>
<ul>
<li>3 tbsp chia seeds</li>  
  <li>1 cup almond milk (or any milk)</li>
  <li>½ tsp vanilla extract</li>
  <li>½ cup fruit topping</li>
</ul>
 <p> Stir chia chia seeds, milk, and vanilla. Refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight.Stir and top with fruit before serving.</p>

  <h2>Whole Grain Pancakes</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>½ cup whole-wheat flour</li>
    <li>1 tsp baking powder</li>
    <li>1 egg</li>
    <li>½ cup milk</li>
    <li>1 tsp honey</li>
    <li>½ tsp oil for cooking</li>
  </ul>
<p>Mix flour and baking powder in a bowl. Whisk in egg, milk, and honey. Heat pan with oil. Cook pancakes (¼ cup batter each) 2–3 min per side.</p>
